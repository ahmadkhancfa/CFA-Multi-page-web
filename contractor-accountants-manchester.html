Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you advise on IR35 status for my contracts?

Yes. We provide IR35 guidance for contractors operating inside or outside IR35, helping you understand your status and structure your arrangements correctly to remain compliant.

Should I operate through a limited company as a contractor?

For many contractors, a limited company structure offers significant tax efficiency. We'll assess your situation and help you decide whether a limited company, umbrella arrangement or self-employment is most appropriate.

Do you support contractors across Manchester and the North West?

Yes. We support contractors and freelancers across Manchester, Greater Manchester and the wider North West including Salford, Trafford and Stockport.

What does your contractor accounting package include?

Our contractor packages cover limited company accounts, self-assessment, VAT returns, payroll, dividend planning and IR35 advice — all for a fixed monthly fee.
