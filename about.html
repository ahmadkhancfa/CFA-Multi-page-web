About CFA

Manchester's Trusted Financial Architects

Founded on the principles of integrity and technical excellence, CFA Accountants has become a cornerstone of Manchester's professional services sector. Our team consists of senior ACCA-accredited professionals who bring decades of combined experience across diverse industries including tech, hospitality, real estate, solicitors firms and construction.

Our Story

Built on Relationships, Driven by Results

CFA Accountants was founded with a simple belief: that great accounting is about more than numbers. It's about understanding your business, anticipating challenges, and being there when it matters most. Based in Manchester and serving clients across the UK.
