Privacy Policy

How CFA Accountants Ltd collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

CFA Accountants Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is a company registered in England, Company Number 16156596, with its registered office at Salford Innovation Forum, 51 Frederick Rd, Salford, M6 6FP. We are the data controller responsible for your personal data.

2. Information we collect

We may collect and process the following information about you:

  • Contact details such as your name, email address and phone number, including information you submit through our contact form.
  • Financial and business information you provide to us in order for us to deliver our accounting and advisory services.
  • Technical data such as your IP address and browser type, collected automatically when you visit our website.

3. How we use your information

We use your personal data to respond to enquiries, provide our services, meet our legal and regulatory obligations, and to improve our website and communications. We process your data on the lawful bases of contract, legitimate interests, legal obligation and, where relevant, consent.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We do not outsource our work overseas. We may share information with trusted software providers (such as our cloud accounting platforms) and with HMRC and other regulators where required by law, always under appropriate safeguards.

5. Data retention

We keep your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for, including to satisfy any legal, accounting or reporting requirements.

6. Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have rights including the right to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please email cfa@cfaaccountants.com or call +44 7440 290584.
