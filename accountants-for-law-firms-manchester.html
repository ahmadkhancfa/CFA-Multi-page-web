Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you understand SRA Accounts Rules?

Yes. We have specific experience working with law firms on SRA Accounts Rules compliance, including client money controls, reconciliations and record keeping requirements.

Can you handle client money reconciliations for our legal practice?

Yes. We support law firms with client account reconciliations, internal controls and the record keeping standards required under SRA regulations.

Do you work with solicitors and law firms across Manchester?

Yes. We support legal practices across Manchester, Greater Manchester and the wider North West including Salford, Trafford and Stockport.

What accounting software do you use for law firms?

We use cloud accounting systems including Xero, which integrate cleanly with legal practice management software. We set up and manage the full workflow for you.
