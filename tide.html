FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Tide? add
Tide is a business financial platform — not a bank — designed to help small businesses, freelancers and contractors manage their finances. Business accounts are provided by ClearBank.
How do I open a Tide account? add
You can apply online in under 10 minutes using the links on this page. No impact on your credit score, and our referral code CFAACTSF2F is applied automatically.
Can I form a company and open an account together? add
Yes. Tide offers a streamlined process to register a new limited company and open a Tide business account at the same time — ideal for startups.
Is Tide a bank? add
Tide is a business financial platform, not a bank. Business accounts are provided by ClearBank Ltd, authorised by the FCA and PRA (Financial Services Register number 754568).
How can CFA Accountants help? add
As a Tide partner, we can introduce eligible businesses to Tide and handle the accounting, bookkeeping, payroll and tax that goes alongside your account. Get in touch and we'll guide you through it.
How do I contact Tide support? add
You can reach Tide's support team directly through the Tide app or via tide.co.
