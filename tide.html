Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tide? add
Tide is a business financial platform that provides financial tools and business accounts for UK businesses.
How do I open a Tide account? add
You can apply online through our Tide referral link. Applications can usually be completed in minutes.
Can I form a company and open an account together? add
Yes. Tide offers an option to register a company and open a business account as part of the same process.
Is Tide a bank? add
No. Tide is a business financial platform, not a bank. Tide offers business accounts provided by ClearBank Ltd.
How can CFA Accountants help? add
We support startups and growing businesses with company formation, bookkeeping, payroll, VAT, tax compliance and ongoing accounting services.
How do I contact Tide support? add
Tide provides in-app support and customer service through its platform.
