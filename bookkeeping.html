Compliance

Bookkeeping for HMRC Compliance

Maintaining structured financial records is a core operational requirement. HMRC is increasingly moving towards mandatory digital record-keeping, meaning businesses must track income and expenses using compatible accounting software.

Our service ensures your documentation satisfies these changing regulations. We establish clear digital links across your accounting data so your figures are fully verifiable, reducing compliance risk during routine VAT inspections or self-assessment reviews.

How It Works

Our Systematic Bookkeeping Process

We manage the technical onboarding and handle your ongoing data entry weekly — an organised, low-friction workflow built around your business.

1

System configuration

We set up your business profile on secure cloud platforms such as Xero, then connect secure bank feeds to automate the daily import of your business sales and expenditure.

2

Digital document capture

Ledger reconciliation

Client Case Study

Ancoats Hospitality Sector

Optimising Record-Keeping for a Local Café (client anonymised for privacy)

The context: A busy independent coffee shop and takeaway venue in Ancoats was managing high volumes of physical supplier invoices alongside complex weekly point-of-sale cash reports. The manual reconciliation process was causing tracking delays and data mismatches before VAT deadlines.

The intervention: We moved the business away from manual bookkeeping files, integrating their electronic point-of-sale (EPOS) till data directly with Xero and setting up automated scanning workflows for their wholesale kitchen invoices.

The result: The business reduced its internal administrative workload, created a verifiable digital audit trail for all operational expenses, and established predictable, stress-free quarterly reporting cycles.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the current Making Tax Digital rules for sole traders? +
Under current government timelines, the HMRC Making Tax Digital for Income Tax guidelines apply to self-employed individuals and landlords with total business income over £50,000 from April 2026. This framework requires businesses to maintain digital records and file digital summary updates every quarter.
Can I still raise invoices manually if I move to cloud software? +
You can, but moving to a digital system lets you generate and email professional invoices directly from your phone or desktop. The system tracks payments automatically, which significantly reduces the manual paperwork involved in chasing outstanding debts.
